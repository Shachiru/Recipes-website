SALAD

Ingredients

  • 3 ears of corn (2 cups corn kernels)
  • 1 pound mini cucumbers (or 1 large English cucumber, sliced)
  • 1 pound cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 3 medium-large avocados, cubed
  • 3 green onion sprigs, finely chopped
  • Zest and juice of 1 lime
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

Step 1
In a large bowl, combine the corn kernels, cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, avocados, and green onions.

Step 2
In a small bowl, whisk together the lime zest, lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

Step 3
Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.

Step 4
Serve immediately or chill for 30 minutes before serving.
