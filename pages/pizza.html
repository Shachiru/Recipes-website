PIZZA

Ingredients

  1. For the Dough:
    • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour (or a mix of all-purpose and whole wheat flour)
    • 1 teaspoon dry active yeast
    • ½ cup warm water (+2 tbsp. if needed)
    • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
    • ⅓ teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon sugar

  2. For the Sauce:
    • 400 grams tomatoes (finely chopped or pureed)
    • 2 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
    • 1 teaspoon - red chili flakes
    • 1 teaspoon - Italian herbs (or dried oregano)
    • ¼ teaspoon - salt
    • ½ teaspoon - black pepper
    • 1 ½ tablespoons - olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon - sugar

  3. For the Toppings:
    • 200 grams mozzarella cheese (grated)
    • 1 ½ cups mixed vegetables (sliced or cubed, such as bell peppers, onions, olives, tomatoes, mushrooms)
    • ½ teaspoon - Italian herbs (or oregano)
    • ¼ teaspoon - red chili flakes (optional)

Instructions

Prepare the dough
  • In a bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  • In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  • Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 10 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic.
  • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours or until it doubles in size.

Prepare the sauce
  • In a saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  • Add the chopped tomatoes, red chili flakes, Italian herbs, salt, pepper, and sugar. Simmer for about 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens.

Assemble the Pizza
  • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  • Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two equal parts. Roll each part into a circle or your desired shape.
  • Place the rolled dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread a layer of the prepared sauce over the dough.
  • Sprinkle the grated mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. Add your favorite toppings and sprinkle with Italian herbs and red chili flakes.

Bake the Pizza
  • Bake in the preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
