FRIED RICE

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons - oil (vegetable or sesame)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (like peas, carrots, and corn)
  • 2 cups cooked white rice (preferably day-old)
  • 3 eggs, whisked
  • ¾ cup - green onions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon - soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon - oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon - sesame oil (optional)
  • ¼ teaspoon - white pepper

Instructions

Step 1
Prepare the Ingredients: Make sure all your ingredients are ready to go, as the cooking process is quick.

Step 2
Cook the Vegetables: Heat the oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and onion, and sauté until fragrant and the onion is translucent.

Step 3
Add Vegetables: Add the mixed vegetables and cook for a few minutes until they are tender.

Step 4
Cook the Eggs: Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet. Pour the whisked eggs into the other side and scramble them until fully cooked.

Step 5
Add the Rice: Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Stir everything together.

Step 6
Season: Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil (if using), and white pepper. Stir well to combine all the flavors.

Step 7
Finish with Green Onions: Stir in the green onions and cook for another minute or two until everything is heated through.
