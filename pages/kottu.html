CHICKEN KOTTU

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons - Vegetable Oil
  • ½ - Medium Onion
  • 1 tablespoon - Garlic (minced, about 3 garlic cloves)
  • 1 tablespoon - Ginger (minced)
  • 6 - Large Jalapenos (or 3 anaheim peppers, halved and sliced, see recipe notes for substitutions)
  • 1 cup - Leeks (sliced, washed, white part only)
  • ¼ - Cabbage
  • ½ teaspoon - Cayenne Pepper (leave it out if you prefer a mild kottu)
  • 2 heaped cups - Chicken (shredded, leftover chicken curry OR rotisserie chicken)
  • ½ cup - Leftover Curry Sauce OR Use (the curry spices below)
  • 1 cup - Carrots (grated)
  • 3 - Eggs
  • 1 cup - Cheese (shredded, optional - for chicken and cheese kottu roti)
  • add - Salt And Pepper
  • 1 pound - Godhamba Roti (chopped, or flour tortillas, see recipe notes for substitutions)
  • 1 tablespoon - Sri Lankan Curry Powder
  • ⅓ cup - Chicken Stock
  • ¼ teaspoon - Black Pepper
  • ½ teaspoon - Cayenne Pepper (for a spicy version)
  • ½ teaspoon - Ground Cinnamon
