CHICKEN KOTTU

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons - Vegetable Oil
  • ½ - Medium Onion
  • 1 tablespoon - Garlic (minced, about 3 garlic cloves)
  • 1 tablespoon - Ginger (minced)
  • 6 - Large Jalapenos (or 3 anaheim peppers, halved and sliced, see recipe notes for substitutions)
  • 1 cup - Leeks (sliced, washed, white part only)
  • ¼ - Cabbage
  • ½ teaspoon - Cayenne Pepper (leave it out if you prefer a mild kottu)
  • 2 heaped cups - Chicken (shredded, leftover chicken curry OR rotisserie chicken)
  • ½ cup - Leftover Curry Sauce OR Use (the curry spices below)
  • 1 cup - Carrots (grated)
  • 3 - Eggs
  • 1 cup - Cheese (shredded, optional - for chicken and cheese kottu roti)
  • add - Salt And Pepper
  • 1 pound - Godhamba Roti (chopped, or flour tortillas, see recipe notes for substitutions)
  • 1 tablespoon - Sri Lankan Curry Powder
  • ⅓ cup - Chicken Stock
  • ¼ teaspoon - Black Pepper
  • ½ teaspoon - Cayenne Pepper (for a spicy version)
  • ½ teaspoon - Ground Cinnamon

Instructions

Step 1
Prepare the Ingredients: Make sure all your ingredients are ready to go, as the cooking process is quick.

Step 2
Cook the Aromatics: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, garlic, and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.

Step 3
Add Vegetables: Add the sliced jalapenos, leeks, cabbage, and grated carrots. Cook for a few minutes until the vegetables are tender.

Step 4
Add Chicken and Curry Sauce: Stir in the shredded chicken and leftover curry sauce. Mix well to combine.

Step 5
Scramble the Eggs: Push the mixture to one side of the skillet. Crack the eggs into the empty side and scramble them until fully cooked.

Step 6
Combine Everything: Mix the scrambled eggs with the rest of the ingredients in the skillet. Add the chopped roti pieces and stir well.

Step 7
Season and Finish: Add the Sri Lankan curry powder, chicken stock, black pepper, and ground cinnamon. Stir everything together and cook for a few more minutes until heated through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Step 8
Optional Cheese: If using cheese, sprinkle it over the top and let it melt before serving.
